1 Introduction

Welcome to 888 Lobiano's Farm Resort. By making a reservation through our system, you agree to comply with these Terms of Service.

2 Reservation Policy

  • All reservations are subject to availability.
  • A reservation is confirmed only after payment verification.
  • Pending reservations without verified payment may be canceled within 24 hours.
  • Reservations for public holidays or peak seasons may require a minimum stay or higher deposit.
  • We do not guarantee specific room types or locations but will do our best to accommodate preferences based on availability.

3 Payment Terms

  • Payments must be made through the provided QR code and verified by our admin.
  • Proof of payment must be uploaded during the reservation process.
  • A minimum of 50% deposit is required to confirm your reservation.
  • The remaining balance must be paid onsite upon check-in.
  • Payments are refundable upon a certain period of time.

4 Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellation can only be made a week after the reservation is made. If the reservation is cancelled after 1 week of booking, a 20% deduction from the reservation fee will apply.
  • If the cancellation is made within 3 days of the reserved date, no refund will be provided.
  • Cancellations must be requested through the reservation system. Any requests made through other channels (e.g., phone, email) will not be considered valid.
  • In case of cancellations within 1 week but after 3 days of the reserved date, a 50% deduction from the reservation fee will apply.
  • No cancellations will be accepted for bookings made less than 48 hours before the reserved date.
  • If a reservation is cancelled due to extenuating circumstances (e.g., medical emergencies), a partial refund may be considered upon review, subject to management approval.
  • If a guest does not show up for the reserved date, no refund will be issued and the full reservation fee will be forfeited.
  • All refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 3–10 business days.
  • If the resort cancels your reservation due to unforeseen circumstances (e.g., maintenance issues, natural disasters), you will be fully refunded.

5 Rescheduling Policy

  • Rescheduling requests must be made at least a week before the reserved date.
  • Rescheduling requests made less than a week before the reserved date will not be accepted.
  • Rescheduling is allowed only once per reservation.
  • Rescheduling is subject to availability of the requested new date.
  • If rescheduled, the original reservation fee may be adjusted according to the new booking date's pricing.

6 Guest Responsibilities

  • Guests are expected to maintain a respectful and quiet environment to ensure other guests' comfort.
  • The resort reserves the right to evict guests for disruptive behavior without a refund.
